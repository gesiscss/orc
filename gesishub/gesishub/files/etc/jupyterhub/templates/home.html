Import

          
Enter your repository information in the Binder form. We build and launch your binder-ready repository like MyBinder.org.

        

      

    

    

      

        
        

          
Persistent Environment

          
Once a repository has been built it will be added to the list of your projects. Your projects have a persistent $HOME directory saving your work in the Jupyter environment.

        

      

    

    

      

        
        

          
Open Science

          
The service supports the publication of reproducible and executable analysis workflows. GESIS Notebooks is freely available and builds on open-source software.
